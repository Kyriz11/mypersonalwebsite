"My Experiences"

Last semester when I had to do my Industrial Training, I applied to do my LI at Perpustakaan Tun Sri Lanang (PTSL), UKM Bangi. I worked there for a total of 6 weeks. For the first 2 weeks I was assigned to explore all the units at PTSL and other library branches in UKM. For the third week, I got assigned permanently to the Archive and Special Collections. So, for the entire remaining month I worked there.

It was a blast as I had attended a few programs. For example, Gotong-royong, learning while visiting Academy Jawi, and a few other lectures.
